About

The developer clients come back to

I'm Inam ul Haq — a Shopify theme developer based in Punjab, Pakistan, with over a decade of hands-on storefront implementation experience. I've worked across freelance platforms, two agencies (Dubai and India), and long-term direct client retainers.

My work covers the full implementation layer: custom Liquid theme development, Figma and PSD to pixel-perfect Shopify, Online Store 2.0 section architecture, multi-market builds via Shopify Markets, and complex bug diagnosis and resolution.

I don't claim marketing outcomes. What I deliver is clean, maintainable theme code that works exactly as designed, ships on time, and doesn't create problems for the developers who work on it after me.

Clients return for a specific reason: I solve Shopify theme problems other developers walk away from — often within the same session.
